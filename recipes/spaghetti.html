Million Dollar Spaghetti


Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package spaghetti
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (28 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1/2 cup butter, sliced
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 12 minutes. Drain.
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef and onion in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain and discard grease. Stir spaghetti sauce into skillet. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Stir butter, sour cream, and cream cheese into spaghetti until evenly combined. Pour half the spaghetti mixture into a baking dish. Top with half the meat sauce. Repeat layers.
  5. Bake in preheated oven until bubbly, about 30 minutes. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese and parsley over the top.
